# DevX Annual Meeting: December 15, 2025

The DevX Workgroup meeting will take place in Lille this year.
The meeting has three objectives:  
1. Integrating newcomers to the community and learning about their DevX issues.  
2. Continue discussing the workgroup's forward-looking aspects.  
3. Discuss potential projects and collaborations.

**Public transport:**

You can reach the location from the city center or the train station via the subway, cf. [Ilévia](https://www.ilevia.fr/horaires?query=ILEVIA%20M%C3%A9tro%20M1&id=line:TRA:ME1&type=line).
Take the M1 line (yellow) towards 4 Cantons Grand Stade and get off at the last stop.
From there, you can either walk east for about 15 minutes through the university campus or take the bus.
If you choose to take the bus, take lines 66 or 72 and get off at Verger or Haley.
